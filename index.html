Expand

The song recommender through face detection system described in this paper utilizes computer vision algorithms to analyze facial expressions in real-time. The system incorporates deep learning, collaborative filtering, and user feedback to provide personalized and emotionally resonant music recommendations. Through extensive experiments, the system's effectiveness is demonstrated, highlighting its potential to revolutionize music recommendation systems by incorporating visual cues for a more engaging and immersive musical experience.
